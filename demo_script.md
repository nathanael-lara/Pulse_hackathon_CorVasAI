# CorVas AI Pitch Script

## 3-Minute Demo Outline

### Opening

- We built **CorVas AI**.
- CorVas AI turns cardiac rehab from a discharge instruction into a guided recovery journey.
- Our focus is the real problem behind rehab failure:
- patients do not fail because the science is weak
- patients fail because support disappears once they get home
- Maria does not need another tracker
- she needs clarity, motivation, and a safety net

---

### The Problem

- Cardiac rehab works
- but most patients never fully engage
- the barriers are emotional, logistical, and behavioral
- fear
- confusion
- missed meds
- skipped rehab
- no ride
- no reinforcement
- every missed day increases the chance the patient drops off completely

---

### Our Core Pitch

- CorVas AI is an AI-powered cardiac recovery companion
- it helps patients:
- understand what the doctor said
- stay on track with meds and rehab
- get support before setbacks become emergencies
- escalate to humans when risk becomes real

---

### Demo Moment 1: The Win

- Here is the first required AI moment
- Maria finishes her walk
- CorVas AI recognizes the progress
- it responds with grounded encouragement based on her rehab plan
- not generic wellness language
- not made-up medical advice
- it reinforces:
- you completed today’s activity
- you stayed in a safe range
- you are moving forward in recovery

---

### Demo Moment 2: The Wall

- Here is the second required AI moment
- Maria is tired, anxious, and skipped rehab
- this is where most patients disengage
- CorVas AI does three things immediately:
- explains what matters in simple language
- gives the next best step
- escalates if the pattern looks risky
- instead of shame, the patient gets support
- instead of silence, the care circle gets visibility

---

### What Makes This Different

- this is not a dashboard
- this is not just reminders
- this is a full recovery support system
- one place for:
- live visit explanations
- medication adherence
- 12-week rehab progression
- document understanding
- family and transport support

---

### The Hook: Why Patients Stay Engaged at Week 8

- our hook is **supportive accountability**
- by Week 8, fear fades
- motivation drops
- routines break
- CorVas AI keeps patients engaged through:
- a structured 12-week rehab plan
- visible progress over time
- emotionally supportive nudges
- family and peer reinforcement
- transport and care matching when logistics become the blocker
- the patient always knows:
- what happened
- what is next
- who can help

---

### Product Walkthrough

- **Visit**
- live transcript
- plain-language AI explanation
- pre-visit prep
- post-visit follow-up

- **Medications**
- reminders
- adherence tracking
- missed-dose escalation

- **Rehab**
- interactive 12-week plan
- weekly milestones
- progress and backlog history

- **Documents**
- labs
- prescriptions
- summaries in plain language

- **Family & Support**
- care circle
- provider matching
- community transport support

---

### AI Safety

- one of our most important design choices:
- the AI does not invent diagnoses
- it stays grounded in:
- physician guidance
- visit transcript context
- the prescribed rehab plan
- when risk rises, the product hands off to humans
- care team
- family support
- matched providers

---

### The Blueprint

- here is how the system works
- inputs:
- visit transcript
- medication behavior
- rehab progress
- wearable-style signals like heart rate and steps
- those signals flow into CorVas AI
- the AI layer:
- explains
- supports
- nudges
- watches for red flags
- the escalation layer routes concern to real humans
- that is the full loop:
- patient
- context
- AI support
- human intervention

---

### Closing

- CorVas AI helps patients not just hear care instructions
- but actually follow through at home
- we built for the hardest part of recovery:
- the days after discharge
- when motivation fades
- questions build up
- and small misses turn into major setbacks
- CorVas AI brings back clarity, continuity, and human-supported recovery
